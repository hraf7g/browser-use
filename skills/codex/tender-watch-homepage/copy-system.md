# Copy System

Use this when writing or editing Tender Watch homepage copy.

## Tone

- Premium
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Operational
- Clear

Write like software for serious regional operators, bid teams, and commercial leads.

## Positioning

Tender Watch is:

- an AI-powered tender monitoring platform
- for MENA companies
- using browser automation on official procurement sources
- to detect, analyze, match, and alert

## Always Emphasize

- official sources
- browser automation
- Arabic and English tender handling
- company-profile matching
- operational alerts and daily briefs
- MENA-wide relevance

## Avoid

- military or intelligence phrasing
- “spy”, “intel”, “surveillance”, “command center”, “war room”
- mystical AI wording
- “our AI thinks”, “our AI understands like a human”, “our AI never sleeps”
- fake urgency
- fake precision
- unsupported social proof

## Preferred Vocabulary

Use words like:

- official procurement sources
- tender pages
- contract opportunity
- extract
- analyze
- match
- company profile
- country scope
- industry scope
- instant alert
- daily brief
- monitored sources

## CTA Rules

Use only real actions available in the product:

- Start Monitoring
- Create Account
- Sign In
- Browse Tenders

Avoid dead-end CTAs like:

- Book demo
- Talk to sales
- Join waitlist

Unless those routes truly exist and are wired.

## Message Structure

### Hero

Should establish:

- what the product is
- how it works
- why it matters

### Supporting sections

Should answer:

- how the system browses and extracts
- how Arabic/English content is handled
- how profile matching works
- how teams receive results

## Copy Patterns

### Strong

- “Tender Watch browses official procurement sites across MENA, extracts new opportunities, and alerts your team when a tender fits your company profile.”
- “Read Arabic and English tender content, structure the key fields, and send the right opportunities to the right team.”
- “Move from manual portal checking to a monitored flow of matched contracts.”

### Weak

- “AI that transforms procurement forever.”
- “Unlock intelligence at the speed of innovation.”
- “The smartest autonomous tender brain.”

## Arabic Readiness

- Keep source English copy structurally simple so Arabic translation stays strong.
- Avoid idioms and slang.
- Avoid long chains of metaphors.
- Favor compact sentences and explicit nouns.

## Trust Markers

Good trust markers:

- official-source monitoring
- bilingual content handling
- explicit match criteria
- real product surfaces

Bad trust markers:

- fake counts
- fake logos
- fake testimonials
- fake ROI numbers

## Homepage Promise

A good Tender Watch homepage should promise:

- we monitor official tender sources across MENA
- we analyze the tender content in the languages it appears
- we match opportunities to your company’s profile and scope
- we deliver clear alerts without forcing your team to keep checking portals manually
