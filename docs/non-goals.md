# Non-Goals: UAE Tender Watch (UTW)

## 1. Explicit Exclusions
To maintain product focus and solo-founder feasibility, UTW **WILL NOT**:
*   **Auto-Bidding**: Never attempt to submit a bid on behalf of a user.
*   **Proposal Generation**: No "Automatic RFP response" drafting (v1 focus is purely on *intelligence* and *discovery*).
*   **Legal / Compliance Interpretation**: Avoid any "Should I bid?" or "Is this legal?" advice. AI will only *summarize*, not *interpret*.
*   **Payment & Financial Workflows**: No handling of tender bond payments, document fees, or bid security within the app.
*   **Multi-Region Support**: Strictly focused on the UAE (Federal + 7 Emirates). No Saudi, Qatar, or wider GCC in v1.
*   **Enterprise Integration**: No deep CRM (Salesforce/Odoo) integration for v1. Dashboard/Email only.
*   **Advanced AI Agents**: No multi-agent reasoning. Simple extraction, summarization, and matching.
*   **Fake Login / Account Bypassing**: We only monitor what is *publicly navigable*. If a portal requires a commercial login to see a title, we don't scrape it for the generic feed.

## 2. Rationale
*   **Financial Risk**: Allowing auto-bidding or bond payments introduces massive legal and financial liability for a solo founder.
*   **Product Clarity**: UTW is an *Intelligence* layer, not a *Bidding* tool. Our goal is to solve the discovery problem first.
*   **Stealth & Safety**: Avoiding logins and bypassing makes the scapers lower-risk for anti-bot detection and legal challenges.
